    • About us

    The Jharkhand state government is devoted to advancing innovation and excellence in education. According to this goal, we are creating an online platform that is integrated so that all colleges and universities in Jharkhand can share details about the projects that their students have completed. This platform will be a valuable tool for academics, researchers, and business people alike.

    The platform will have a number of features, including:

    • A searchable archive of student work from all Jharkhand universities and colleges.

    • The capability for students to share their projects with others by uploading them to the site.

    • Tools for preventing and detecting plagiarism.

    • Forums where students can talk about their initiatives and work with others.

    We believe that this platform will play a significant role in promoting innovation and excellence in education in Jharkhand. By providing students with a platform to share their work and collaborate with others, we are helping them to develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the 21st century.
